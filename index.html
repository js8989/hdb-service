HDB Service

A prototype of a UK government digital benefit service — simulating PIP (Personal Independence Payment) — built in the GOV.UK design system.

Citizen facing

Registration journey

The end-to-end journey a citizen goes through to apply for HDB — from the start page through to submitting an application and optionally creating an account. Includes address lookup with postcode matching and a passkey setup flow.
