Benefits & financial support

Apply for Health and Disability Benefit (HDB)

Get financial support if you have a long-term health condition or disability that affects your daily life or ability to work.

Use this service to:

  • check if you're eligible for Health and Disability Benefit
  • find out how much you could receive
  • submit a new claim or update an existing one

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your National Insurance number (if you have one)
  • details of your health condition or disability
  • information about any healthcare professionals treating you
  • your bank account details (for payment)
!Applying takes around 30 minutes. You cannot save your progress and return later.
Start now

If you need help applying, call the HDB helpline on 0800 328 5644 (free from a landline or mobile). Lines are open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm.

By using this service you agree to the terms of use and our privacy policy.

Apply for Health and Disability Benefit

You can still apply without a National Insurance number

Not having a National Insurance number will not stop you from applying for Health and Disability Benefit.

However, if your application is successful, you will need to get a National Insurance number within 6 months of your award date.

Important: If you do not get a National Insurance number within 6 months of a successful decision, your benefit will be reviewed and may stop.

This only applies if your application is successful. You do not need to do anything now.

On the next page we will ask if you would like to apply for a National Insurance number as part of this application. This can make the process easier.

Create your HDB account

Manage your claim online

Creating a free HDB account means you can:

  • track the progress of your application in real time
  • see decisions and letters as soon as they are issued
  • request a mandatory reconsideration if you disagree with a decision
  • save and return to forms without losing your progress
  • update your contact details and address
  • get reminders about appointments and deadlines

Your account will be linked to the application you have just submitted. You will not need to enter your details again.

Set up a passkey for faster, safer sign-in

A passkey lets you sign in using your device's built-in security — like your fingerprint, face recognition or screen lock PIN. You do not need to remember a password.
